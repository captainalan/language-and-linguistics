Language and Linguistics

Hello! Welcome to my page on language and linguistics. Here, I have content related to many languages I have experience studying, speaking, reading, and writing as well as articles and notes on theories of language and communication, more broadly. Naturally, this will include anthropological and sociological armchair theorizing...

Works in Progress

Currently, I'm developing individual language pages for various languages I've studied. The content of these pages is largely anecdotal and opinion based, reading somewhat like a product review. I write in this way because there are many, many languages out there one might choose to study, and nobody has time to study them all. So, I do some "market research" here to help you choose what is and isn't worth your time!

Here, I also plan to link book reviews I write on content related to language/linguistics. Primarily, I plan to do that through goodreads.
