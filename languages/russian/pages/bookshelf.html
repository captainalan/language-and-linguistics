Bookshelf

I'm currently using the following books:

  • The New Penguin Russian Course: A Complete Course for Beginners (Nicholas J. Brown, 1996)
  • Russian Practical Dictionary (Dmitry Yermolovich, 2nd ed. 2015)
  • Roots of the Russian Language: An Elementary Guide to wordbuilding (George Z. Patrick, 1989)
  • Modern Russian Grammar: A Practical Guide (John Dunn and Shamil Khairov, 2009)
  • Lonely Planet Phrasebook
  • Oxford Beginner's Russian Dictionary—this volume is slim enough such that you can thumb through it and quickly identify any common vocabulary you don't now. Obviously not comprehensive, but useful for a starting word list and the concise grammar info
