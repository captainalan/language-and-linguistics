Technology

Tech support for Spanish is overall pretty good because Spanish is a widely used language that uses the Latin alphabet. Some things are suboptimal, like keyboard layouts—they are pretty much all based on QWERTY, with lots of hard to reach buttons in silly places (also, rarely used letters like k are in easy to reach places). Uso emacs para teclar en español.
